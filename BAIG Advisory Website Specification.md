## **BAIG Advisory Website — Final Build & Planning Specification** 

## **1. Document Purpose** 

This document is the final planning and build specification for the BAIG Advisory website. 

It is intended to guide website design, content structure, development, and AI-assisted editing through Codex or any other developer workflow. 

The purpose of the website is not simply to display information. The purpose is to create a premium executive-facing digital presence for Jawad Baig and BAIG Advisory that builds immediate credibility, communicates high-level advisory capability, and encourages serious prospects to make contact. 

This document should be treated as the source of truth for the homepage build unless new approved business content is provided later. 

## **2. Core Website Purpose** 

## **2.1 Primary Purpose** 

The website must position BAIG Advisory as a premium executive advisory and consulting firm led by Jawad Baig. 

The website should make a senior viewer think: 

Jawad Baig has real executive experience, has operated at a high level, understands strategy, technology, finance, governance, compliance, AI, and international markets, and is credible enough to advise senior leaders, boards, and organizations. 

The site should earn the first conversation. 

It does not need to close the full advisory engagement on the website. It needs to make a skeptical, timepoor executive willing to engage. 

## **2.2 Core Positioning Thesis** 

The central positioning idea is: 

1 

Practitioner-level insight, not theory. 

This means BAIG Advisory should not be presented as a generic consulting firm or a freelancer-style advisory page. 

The website must show that Jawad Baig has personally operated in the types of environments clients are navigating. 

Every content decision should support this idea. 

## **2.3 Target Viewer** 

The target viewer is a senior decision-maker or someone close to a senior decision-maker. 

Primary audience: 

- CEOs 

- CFOs 

- CIOs 

- Board members 

- Founders 

- Senior executives 

- Enterprise transformation leaders 

- Senior leaders in US, GCC, and European markets 

The viewer should feel that this site is written for a serious executive audience, not for general consumers. 

## **2.4 Desired Impression** 

The site must create these impressions: 

1. Executive credibility 

2. Trust 

3. Operational experience 

4. Strategic clarity 

5. International market awareness 

6. Premium advisory positioning 

7. Modern competence, especially around digital transformation and AI governance 

8. Professional restraint 

The site should feel confident, not loud.
